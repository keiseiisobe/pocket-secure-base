# DeepBreath: The Navigator Whale

DeepBreath is a mobile exploration partner for **neurodivergent individuals** (ASD, ADHD, and Sensory Sensitive people). It acts as a reliable, calm vessel—a "Navigator Whale"—that carries users through the unpredictable "ocean" of the outside world.

## The Concept
Based on the **Secure Base** theory, DeepBreath bridges the gap between islands of human-created safety (Home/School). By using real-time environmental data and AI, it reduces the "sensory friction" of the city, empowering users to explore the world with the same confidence they feel at home.

<img src="docs/assets/concept_map.svg" alt="DeepBreath: The Navigator Whale" width="600">

## Key Services
*   **Stressless Navigation**: AI-powered routing that prioritizes quiet, low-crowd paths over speed.
*   **Verbal Guardian**: Real-time audio feedback for situational awareness and emotional grounding.
*   **Panic & SOS Support**: One-tap access to sensory relaxation tools, "Safe Haven" routing, and caregiver alerts.
*   **Reliable Edge AI**: On-device "Local Guardian" ensuring safety features work 100% offline in subway tunnels.

## Getting Started
1. `flutter pub get`
2. Run on your preferred mobile device.
