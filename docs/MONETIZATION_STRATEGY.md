# Monetization Strategy: Sustaining the Secure Base

## 1. Overview
The "Pocket Secure Base" is a mission-driven application. Our monetization strategy is designed to ensure the service remains accessible to those who need it most while building a sustainable business model through institutional and public-sector partnerships.

---

## 2. Revenue Streams

### A. B2C: The "Safety Upgrade" (Freemium Model)
We offer the core navigation and sensory map for free, while charging for high-resource "Guardian" features.
- **Free Tier**: 
    - Sensory-aware route planning (Cloud-based).
    - Access to the community sensory map.
    - Basic haptic grounding tools.
- **Premium Subscription ("The Guardian Pass")**:
    - **Offline Local AI**: Access to `gemma-3-1b` and `Kokoro-82m` for 100% offline safety.
    - **Advanced Monitoring**: Real-time ambient noise detection and tactical voice intervention.
    - **Personalized Comfort Zones**: Ability to save and share unlimited "Safe Havens."

### B. B2B2C: Institutional Licensing
The primary revenue driver. We partner with the organizations that already provide the "Primary Secure Base."
- **Social Welfare Organizations**: Bulk licenses for social workers to provide to their clients as part of their support package.
- **Special Education Schools**: Integration into "Life Skills" curricula for students learning to navigate the city independently.
- **Corporate DEI (Diversity, Equity & Inclusion)**: Licensing the tool for neurodivergent employees to assist with their commutes to the office.

### C. B2G: Municipal & Transit Partnerships
Partnering with city governments and transit authorities (e.g., Tokyo Metro).
- **Incentivized Navigation**: Cities pay a "Public Accessibility Fee" to make the app free for residents, reducing the burden on public transport escorts and emergency services.
- **Infrastructure Insights**: Providing de-identified, aggregated "Sensory Hotspot" data to city planners to identify areas that need noise mitigation or better lighting.

### D. Insurance & Healthcare
- **Risk Mitigation**: Partnering with health insurance providers who benefit from the increased autonomy and reduced anxiety-related incidents of their policyholders.

---

## 3. Ethical Constraints (The "Secure Base" Promise)
To maintain the trust of our users, we adhere to strict monetization ethics:
1.  **No Data Selling**: We never sell individual user location or stress data.
2.  **No "Panic" Paywalls**: Core SOS and basic grounding tools are always free.
3.  **Privacy First**: All monetization happens at the service level, never by exploiting user vulnerability.
