# Core Services: DeepBreath

## 1. Core Commitment: Neural Reliability
As described in [CONCEPT.md](./CONCEPT.md), the primary value proposition of DeepBreath is to provide absolute **reliability** during excursions. We achieve this through a **Scientific Approach** (Neuroscience & Psychology) that treats navigation as a problem of **Neural Regulation**.

By providing a predictable "Navigator Whale," we help the user's brain mitigate environmental stress through real-time interventions that mimic clinical psychotherapy.

---

## 2. The Science of Our Services

### A. Predictive Sensory Mitigation (Strategic Navigation)
*Mechanism: Minimizing "Prediction Error" (Free Energy Principle) for the user's brain.*
- **Sensory-Aware Routing**: Prioritizing paths that minimize biological "surprisal"—avoiding high-decibel areas and extreme pedestrian density to prevent sensory overstimulation.
- **Environmental Pre-scanning**: Using LLMs to "see through the waves" by analyzing real-time data (ODPT/Search). This removes the "unknown" variables, keeping the user's Prefrontal Cortex (PFC) in control.

### B. Biological Co-Regulation (Immediate Support)
*Mechanism: Using rhythmic tactile/auditory stimuli to signal safety to the Vagus Nerve (Polyvagal Theory).*
- **Haptic Grounding**: Gentle, rhythmic vibration patterns that provide a "tactile heartbeat," using **Deep Pressure Stimulation (DPS)** to increase vagal tone and reduce cortisol.
- **Rhythmic Audio Grounding**: Low-stimulation, steady-rhythm audio assets that provide a biological signal of safety during high-stress moments.
- **The "Safe Haven" Exit**: Immediate routing to pre-verified quiet zones to stop a sensory "shutdown" or "meltdown."

### C. Contextual Safety (Real-Time Feedback)
*Mechanism: Assisting the Hippocampus in distinguishing specific events from generalized threats.*
- **Verbal Guardian**: Real-time audio context (e.g., *"The noise ahead is just construction; we will be past it in 20 meters"*) to prevent "Fear Generalization" and maintain contextual safety.
- **Offline Reliability (Local Guardian)**: On-device AI ensures the "companion" never disappears, providing the constant presence required for "Proximity Maintenance."

### D. Real-Time Cognitive Reframing (Ecological Momentary Intervention)
*Mechanism: Applying Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and Extinction Learning in the moment of stress.*
- **Automatic Interpretation Correction**: Using real-time data to correct automatic negative thoughts (e.g., reframing a train delay as a "standard signal check").
- **Executive Scaffolding**: Taking over the high-energy task of pathfinding during a crisis to preserve the user's **Cognitive Flexibility**.
- **Extinction Learning Support**: Providing the "Safe Context" required for the brain (PFC/Hippocampus) to learn that environmental stimuli (like loud sirens) are not personal threats.
